The novel "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" by J.K. Rowling, written in English and published in the year 1997 is a classic example of fantasy novel.

The novel is set in the United Kingdom and tells the story of an orphaned boy, Harry Potter, who discovers he is a wizard and is sent to Hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry in Scotland. While he discovers the mysteries of the wizarding world, he confronts magical creatures and learns powerful spells. He must also face a series of challenges to defeat the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort, who is trying to steal the Philosopher’s Stone and use it to become immortal. 

One of the most attractive features of the novel is its vivid description of the wizarding world. The characters are artfully designed, the landscape described in full detail and magic used effectively to enhance the story. J K Rowling's writing has captured the imagination of readers all over the world, making the novel one of the best-selling books of all time. 

The novel offers an exciting adventure filled with suspense and thrills. It is not only exciting but also thought-provoking as it delves into themes such as friendship, loyalty and the fight against evil. The book is suitable for readers of all ages as the author's style of writing is easy to comprehend. 

Overall, with its riveting story and strong themes, Philosopher’s Stone is a classic fantasy novel and a must-read for all fans of the genre. It is definitely worth a read and is guaranteed to be a captivating adventure. I give it a rating of 4.5/5 stars.
